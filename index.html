Exploring The World - Katherine's Travels

Curiosity is one of the first traits we express as children. We are curious about the world around us, and even the simplest things can seem miraculous. Growing up in Missouri was a comfortable and safe experience, but it lacked excitement. That feeling of "missing out" and wanting to experience an adventure inspired me to seek places that would fill that emptiness, but help me flourish by being exposed to different cultures.

I recently went on a trip with my family through a group travel company, Expat Explore, where we were able to visit 11 different countries in 17 days. It was absolutely amazing!

  • Italy
  • Vatican City
  • France
  • Monaco
  • Spain
  • Switzerland
